## Overview

Bay assignment is the core workflow in Dashboard Backus. This guide explains how to assign trucks to bays using drag-and-drop, understand validation rules, and handle special cases.

<Note>
  Bay assignment is only available to **Admin** users. Client users have view-only access.
</Note>

## Bay Configuration

Dashboard Backus manages **10 bays** across the Backus yard:

| Bay ID | Name | Location | Specialization |
|--------|------|----------|----------------|
| **b0.1** | Bahía 0.1 | Left side, middle | General discharge |
| **b0.2** | Bahía 0.2 | Left side, lower | General discharge |
| **b1** | Bahía 1 | Top left | General discharge |
| **b2** | Bahía 2 | Top center-left | General discharge |
| **b3** | Bahía 3 | Top center | Finished product (PT) |
| **b4** | Bahía 4 | Top center-right | Finished product (PT) |
| **b5** | Bahía 5 | Top right | Finished product (PT) |
| **b14** | Bahía 14 | Right side, upper | Finished product + empty bottles |
| **b10** | Bahía 10 | Far right, middle | Restricted (coordination required) |
| **b12** | Bahía 12 | Far right, lower | Restricted (coordination required) |

## Assigning Trucks to Bays

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify Truck in Queue">
    Review the truck queue at the bottom of the screen. Each truck card displays:
    - **Plate number** (e.g., "BFW-234")
    - **Truck type** (Parihuelero, Jumbo, Bi-tren, Tolva, Otros)
    - **Operation** (Carga ⬆ or Descarga ⬇)
    - **Product type** (e.g., "PT", "PP", "Fardos")
    - **Wait time** with traffic light color
    - **Incident count** (if any)
    
    <Tip>
      Prioritize trucks with red traffic light status to minimize wait times.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable Help Mode (Optional)">
    Click the lightbulb icon in the header to highlight compatible bays:
    - **Green bays**: Compatible with the truck being dragged
    - **Red bays**: Incompatible due to validation rules
    
    This visual aid helps prevent assignment errors.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Drag Truck to Bay">
    1. Click and hold on the truck card in the queue
    2. Drag the cursor over the desired bay on the yard map
    3. The bay will highlight to indicate drop readiness
    4. Release the mouse button to complete the assignment
    
    <Warning>
      If the assignment violates validation rules, an error toast will appear and the assignment will be rejected.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Special Bays">
    Bays **b10** and **b12** require coordination with the T2 team:
    - A confirmation dialog will appear: "¡ATENCIÓN! Coordina con T2."
    - Click "OK" to proceed with the assignment
    - Click "Cancel" to choose a different bay
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Assignment">
    Once assigned, the truck:
    - Disappears from the queue
    - Appears in the target bay with real-time status
    - Updates the database with bay location and "Descargando" or "Cargando" status
    - Continues tracking wait time with traffic light colors
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Validation Rules

The system enforces three validation rules before allowing bay assignment:

### 1. Bay Availability

<Check>
  **Rule**: A bay must be unoccupied to accept a new truck.
</Check>

**Error Message**: "Bahía ocupada"

**Solution**: Wait for the current truck to complete its operation and exit, or reassign the truck to a different bay.

### 2. Truck Type Compatibility

<Check>
  **Rule**: The truck type must be in the bay's allowed truck list.
</Check>

Each bay accepts specific truck types:

| Truck Type | Code | Compatible Bays |
|------------|------|-----------------|
| **Parihuelero** | P | All bays (b0.1, b0.2, b1-b5, b10, b12, b14) |
| **Jumbo** | J | All except b10, b12 |
| **Bi-tren** | B | All except b10, b12 |
| **Tolva** | T | b0.1, b0.2, b1, b2 only |
| **Otros** | O | All except b10, b12 |

**Error Message**: "Tipo de camión no permitido"

**Solution**: Select a bay that accepts the truck's type (see table above).

<Warning>
  Bays **b10** and **b12** are restricted to **Parihuelero (P)** trucks only.
</Warning>

### 3. Operation and Product Compatibility

<Check>
  **Rule**: The bay must support the truck's operation (Descarga/Carga) AND the specific product type.
</Check>

#### Discharge Operations (Descarga - D)

| Bay Group | Products Accepted |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **b0.1, b0.2, b1, b2** | Fardos, Envases, CPC, PH, MIXD |
| **b3, b4, b5** | PT (Producto Terminado), MIXD |
| **b14** | PT, MIXD |
| **b10, b12** | PT, PP (Polipropileno), MIXD |

#### Charge Operations (Carga - C)

| Bay Group | Products Accepted |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **b0.1, b0.2, b1, b2** | None (discharge only) |
| **b3, b4, b5** | PP, PT, MIXC |
| **b14** | PP, PT, MIXC, ENVLT (Envases Ligeros Terminados) |
| **b10, b12** | PP, PT, MIXC |

**Error Messages**: 
- "Operación no permitida en esta bahía" - The bay doesn't support the operation type
- "Producto no admitido en esta bahía" - The product is not compatible with the bay

**Solution**: Choose a bay that supports both the operation and product type.

<Info>
  **MIXD** (Mixed Discharge) and **MIXC** (Mixed Charge) are wildcard product codes that match any bay supporting discharge or charge operations respectively.
</Info>

## Reassigning Trucks Between Bays

You can move trucks between bays after initial assignment:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Drag from Occupied Bay">
    Click and hold on the truck information inside the occupied bay (not the bay itself).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Drop on Target Bay">
    Drag to the destination bay and release. The same validation rules apply.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Database Update">
    The system automatically updates:
    - Previous bay is marked as available
    - New bay is marked as occupied
    - `bahia_actual` field in database is updated
    - A notification toast confirms the reassignment
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use reassignment to optimize bay utilization when priorities change or incidents occur.
</Tip>

## Automatic vs. Manual Completion

### Simulation Mode

- Trucks **automatically** exit after 8 time units (8 seconds)
- Exit time is added to shift statistics
- No manual intervention required

### Real Mode

- Trucks require **manual completion** by clicking the "✅ Salida" button
- This reflects real-world operations where operators confirm truck departures
- See [Monitoring Operations](/guides/monitoring-operations) for exit workflow

## Common Assignment Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Parihuelero with Finished Product Discharge

```
Truck: Plate BFW-234, Type: Parihuelero (P)
Operation: Descarga (⬇)
Product: PT (Producto Terminado)

✅ Compatible Bays: b3, b4, b5, b10, b12, b14
❌ Incompatible: b0.1, b0.2, b1, b2 (don't accept PT discharge)

Recommended: b3, b4, or b5 (no coordination required)
```

### Scenario 2: Tolva with Bales Discharge

```
Truck: Plate CTM-789, Type: Tolva (T)
Operation: Descarga (⬇)
Product: Fardos

✅ Compatible Bays: b0.1, b0.2, b1, b2
❌ Incompatible: b3-b5, b10, b12, b14 (Tolva not allowed)

Recommended: b0.1 or b0.2 (side bays, good for Tolva access)
```

### Scenario 3: Bi-tren with Polypropylene Charge

```
Truck: Plate BHQ-456, Type: Bi-tren (B)
Operation: Carga (⬆)
Product: PP (Polipropileno)

✅ Compatible Bays: b3, b4, b5, b14
❌ Incompatible: b0.1, b0.2, b1, b2 (no charge operations)
❌ Incompatible: b10, b12 (Bi-tren not allowed)

Recommended: b3, b4, or b5
```

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Prioritize by Wait Time">
    - Assign red-status trucks first to avoid SLA violations
    - Use the "Priority Alert" panel to identify the longest-waiting truck
    - Monitor the traffic light legend for threshold awareness
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Balance Bay Utilization">
    - Distribute trucks across multiple bays when possible
    - Avoid queuing multiple trucks for the same bay
    - Consider geographic layout for efficient yard flow
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Reserve Special Bays">
    - Use b10 and b12 only when necessary (coordination overhead)
    - Save b14 for special cases (ENVLT products)
    - Keep high-capacity bays (b3-b5) available for peak hours
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use Help Mode for Training">
    - Enable Help Mode when training new operators
    - Practice assignments during low-traffic periods
    - Review validation error messages to learn rules
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

### Assignment Rejected Despite Correct Bay

**Cause**: Bay may already be occupied by another user's assignment that hasn't refreshed yet.

**Solution**: Wait 10 seconds for the next polling cycle and try again.

### Truck Reappears in Queue After Assignment

**Cause**: Database update failed due to connection issue.

**Solution**: Check network connection and reassign the truck. Verify in the database that the `estado` field updated to "Descargando" or "Cargando".

### Cannot Drag Truck Card

**Cause**: Session may not be active.

**Solution**: Verify the header shows "Detener" (stop) button. If it shows "Iniciar" (start), start a new session.
